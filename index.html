Cube grid

Cube grid in UE5 is a feature that helps game developers to position and align objects accurately in a 3D space. The cube grid appears as a visual guide with a series of intersecting lines that form a grid of cubes. By using this grid, developers can achieve more precise placement of objects in their game world.

Landscape

Landscape in UE5 (Unreal Engine 5) refers to the environment or terrain that the player interacts with in a video game or virtual world. It includes things like mountains, trees, rocks, water, and other natural features. UE5 has advanced tools for creating and designing landscapes, such as the new Nanite technology which allows for highly detailed geometry and textures, and the Lumen global illumination system which provides realistic lighting and shadows. With these tools, developers can create breathtaking and immersive landscapes that enhance the player's experience.

Quixel

Quixel is a company that creates digital assets and tools for game development and other industries. Their Megascans library offers a vast collection of high-quality 3D assets and textures. Quixel was acquired by Epic Games in 2019, allowing for integration with the Unreal Engine.

Sky Atmosphere

Sky Atmosphere refers to the rendering of a realistic sky in a video game or virtual environment. It includes features such as clouds, sun and moon position, atmospheric scattering, and other effects that create a believable sky. The Unreal Engine 4 and 5 have advanced tools for creating a dynamic and immersive Sky Atmosphere that enhances the overall visual quality of a game or virtual world.
